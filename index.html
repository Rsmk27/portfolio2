Hi, I'm Srinivasa Manikanta Rajapantula.

Hello! I’m Srinivasa Manikanta Rajapantula (RSMK), an enthusiastic Electrical and Electronics Engineering student with a strong curiosity for innovation and technology. I believe engineering is not just about solving problems—it’s about creating smarter, sustainable solutions that can make a real difference in people’s lives.

Over time, I’ve worked on diverse projects ranging from embedded systems and IoT applications to Arduino-based automation, smart energy solutions, and even creative digital tools like websites, bots, and interactive applications. For me, every project is an opportunity to learn, experiment, and push boundaries.

I am particularly interested in:

Green Tech & Smart Energy

Building sustainable energy systems for the future.

Autonomous & Decentralized Grids

Exploring how smart energy networks can self-heal and adapt.

Electronics & Embedded Systems

Designing practical solutions with microcontrollers and sensors.

Web & Creative Tech

Blending engineering with creativity to build interactive platforms.

When I’m not tinkering with circuits or coding, I enjoy learning new tools like Blender for 3D design and experimenting with ways to bring my ideas to life—whether it’s through hardware projects, simulations, or digital creations. My long-term goal is to bridge core engineering with modern innovations, positioning myself at the intersection of electrical systems, smart energy, and technology-driven solutions. I want my work to reflect not just technical skills, but also creativity, responsibility, and impact.
