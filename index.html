About me

I am RSMK, an undergraduate in Electrical & Electronics Engineering with a practical, research-oriented approach. I value engineering traditions — rigorous analysis, testable designs, and clear documentation — while building toward resilient, renewable, and intelligent power systems. My work blends embedded systems, power electronics, and software.

I build things that last: efficient hardware prototypes, clear simulations, and production-ready code. I prefer methodical design: understand requirements, design conservatively, test thoroughly, then iterate. Current interests include autonomous grids, energy-harvesting systems, and edge AI for control.
